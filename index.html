Grand Theft Auto VI heads to the state of Leonida, home to the neon-soaked streets of Vice City and beyond — in the biggest, most immersive evolution of the Grand Theft Auto series yet.

Jason and Lucia have always known the deck is stacked against them. But when an easy score goes wrong, they find themselves on the darkest side of the sunniest place in America, in the middle of a criminal conspiracy stretching across the state of Leonida — forced to rely on each other more than ever if they want to make it out alive.

Inspired by the Bonnie and Clyde era of American crime, GTA VI delivers a grounded, character-driven narrative focused on consequences, loyalty, and survival in a volatile modern world saturated with social media, viral fame, and institutional corruption.

OFFICIAL CHARACTER BIOS FROM ROCKSTAR GAMES
🧔
★ PROTAGONIST
JASON DUVAL
The Keys · Criminal Enforcer
"Another day in paradise, right?"
Jason wants an easy life, but things just keep getting harder. Jason grew up around grifters and crooks. After a stint in the Army trying to shake off his troubled teens, he found himself in the Keys doing what he knows best — working for local drug runners. It might be time to try something new. Meeting Lucia could be the best or worst thing to ever happen to him. Jason knows how he'd like it to turn out, but right now, it's hard to tell.
Playable Army Veteran Drug Runner
👩
★ PROTAGONIST
LUCIA CAMINOS
First Female Lead in Modern GTA
"Only smart moves from here."
Lucia's father taught her to fight as soon as she could walk. Life has been coming at her swinging ever since. Fighting for her family landed her in the Leonida Penitentiary. Sheer luck got her out. More than anything, Lucia wants the good life her mom has dreamed of since their days in Liberty City — but instead of half-baked fantasies, Lucia is prepared to take matters into her own hands. Fresh out of prison and ready to change the odds in her favor, Lucia's committed to her plan — no matter what it takes. A life with Jason could be her way out.
Playable Latina Protagonist Ex-Con
🧓
◆ CRIMINAL BOSS
BRIAN HEDER
Brian's Boat Works & Marina
"Brian's been around long enough to let others do his dirty work."
Brian's a classic drug runner from the golden age of smuggling in the Keys. Still moving product through his boat yard with his third wife, Lori, Brian has an extensive history as a drug runner and currently oversees a network of criminals while keeping his own hands relatively clean. Brian's letting Jason live rent-free at one of his properties — so long as he helps with local shakedowns, and stops by for Lori's sangria once in a while. Housed in his boatyard is a seaplane, his vehicle of choice in his drug-running days.
Mentor Figure Smuggler Boatyard

● ALLY
CAL HAMPTON
Coast Guard Conspiracy Theorist
"What if everything on the internet was true?"
Jason's friend and a fellow associate of Brian's, Cal feels safest hanging at home, snooping on Coast Guard comms with a few beers and some private browser tabs open. The psychopaths are in charge. Get used to it. Cal is at the low tide of America and happy there. Casual paranoia loves company, but his friend Jason has bigger plans. Somewhat of an eccentric, devil-may-care conspiracy theorist entrenched in everyday paranoia — Cal's penchant for eavesdropping on Coast Guard communications ought to make him a useful ally. He's the Lester of GTA 6.
Conspiracy Theorist Intel Source Jason's Ally

◈ VICE CITY LEGEND
BOOBIE IKE
Jack of Hearts · Only Raw Records
"It's all about heart — the Jack of Hearts."
Boobie is a local Vice City legend — and acts like it. One of the few to transform his time in the streets into a legitimate empire spanning real estate, a strip club, and a recording studio — Boobie's all smiles until it's time to talk business. Boobie Ike transcended the rough-and-tumble street lifestyle of his youth and emerged a successful, influential Vice City mogul. His most recent endeavor, Only Raw Records, is his greatest passion, and together with Dre'Quan Priest, Boobie hopes to make a splash in the music industry.
Music Mogul Strip Club Owner Real Estate
🎤
● SUPPORTING
DRE'QUAN PRIEST
Only Raw Records · Aspiring Mogul
"The club money pay for the studio, and the drug money pay for it all."
Even when he was dealing on the streets to make ends meet, breaking into music was always the goal. Now that he's signed the Real Dimez, Dre'Quan's days of booking acts into Boobie's strip club might be numbered as he sets his sights on bigger things. Boobie Ike's business partner and prized recording artist, Dre'Quan doesn't view music as a means to an end — he has a real, enduring passion for the craft, unlike other musician characters in previous GTA games. He's an ambitious hustler to the bone, and will be an asset — or perhaps a liability — for Jason and Lucia.
Music Artist Signed Artist Wildcard

♦ DUO
REAL DIMEZ
Bae-Luxe & Roxy · Only Raw Records
"All my dimes in this club. Meet my twin, make it a dub."
Bae-Luxe and Roxy aka Real Dimez have been friends since high school — girls with the savvy to turn their time shaking down local dealers into cold, hard cash via spicy rap tracks and a relentless social media presence. An early hit single with local rapper DWNPLY took Real Dimez to new heights. Now, after five years and a whole lot of trouble, they're signed to Only Raw Records, hoping lightning can strike twice. Viral videos. Viral hooks. One more hit and the whole city's theirs.
Rap Duo Social Media Signed Artists

◆ SEASONED CRIMINAL
RAUL BAUTISTA
Bank Robber · Always Hunting Talent
"A professional adapts. Life is full of surprises, my friend."
Confidence, charm, and cunning — Raul's a seasoned bank robber always on the hunt for talent ready to take the risks that bring the biggest rewards. Described as both charming and reckless, Raul has led more heists than most have dreamed of — and he's always scouting for the next hungry recruit. Raul's recklessness raises the stakes with every score. Sooner or later, his crew will have to double down or pull their chips from the table. Like Brian Heder, Raul is a seasoned, wizened career criminal set up to be a galvanizing force in the story — and looks set to send Lucia and Jason into their biggest jobs yet.

🌆
VICE CITY
The glamour, hustle, and greed of America captured in a single city. Each neighborhood has something to offer — from the pastel art deco hotels and bright white sands of Ocean Beach, to the bustling panaderías of Little Cuba and the bootleg brands of the Tisha-Wocka flea market, out to the VC Port, the cruise ship capital of the world.
🌴
LEONIDA KEYS
A chain of sun-drenched islands stretching south from the mainland. Home to Brian Heder's boatyard, Jason's residence, and the laid-back criminal networks that run drugs through the labyrinthine waterways. Life here moves slow — until it doesn't.
🌿
GRASSRIVERS
Vast wetlands and swamp country stretching across central Leonida. Inspired by the Florida Everglades, this region is thick with wildlife, hidden criminal operations, and backwater communities far removed from the neon glow of Vice City.
⚓
PORT GELLHORN
An industrial port city on the western coast of Leonida. The lifeblood of the state's commercial shipping industry, Port Gellhorn is also a key node in the region's underground smuggling routes and serves as a gateway for criminal enterprise.
🎰
AMBROSIA
A sprawling entertainment and nightlife district. Ambrosia buzzes with casinos, clubs, and the kind of excess that attracts both opportunity and danger. Its glittering façade masks a complex web of criminal interests and political corruption beneath the surface.
🏔️
MOUNT KALAGA
A towering national park in the northern reaches of Leonida. Dense forests, rugged terrain, and sweeping vistas contrast sharply with Vice City's urban sprawl. Mount Kalaga offers refuge — and isolation — for those who want to disappear from the world entirely.
